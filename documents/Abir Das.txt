Abir Das is an Assistant Professor in the Computer Science and Engineering Department at IIT Kharagpur. Before joining IIT Kharagpur Abir Das was briefly an Assistant professor at IIIT Delhi. Abir Das was a postdoctoral researcher at Boston University under Prof. Kate Saenko before that. Abir Das completed my PhD in Electrical Engineering from the  University of California at Riverside (UCR). Abir Das's research advisor was Dr. Amit Kumar Roy-Chowdhury in the 'Video Computing Group' led by him. Before joinging UCR, Abir Das had done my Bachelor of Electrical Engineering from Jadavpur University, Calcutta, India in the year 2007
